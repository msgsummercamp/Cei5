Regulation (EC) No. 261/2004 is an EU law that protects air passengers. It requires airlines to offer compensation (up to €600) and assistance when flights are cancelled, delayed over 3 hours, or boarding is denied.

The goal: ensure passengers are fairly treated when their travel plans are disrupted.
